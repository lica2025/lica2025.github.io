Log-gases in Caeli Australi

Conference: 4-8 Aug 2025
Research program: 4-15 Aug 2025
at MATRIX Institute, Creswick, Australia
©NASA

The goal of this program is to bring together world-leading specialists on topics contiguous to random matrix theory for the purposes of sharing recent breakthroughs and initiating collaborations across multiple disciplines. Research activities will focus on relations between random matrix theory and the following themes: statistical mechanics of log-gases; machine learning and statistical inference; (free) probability theory; orthogonal polynomials and asymptotic analysis; and integrable systems including Painlevé equations.

Registration

Registration for the conference is open till May 1st 2025. There is no registration fee.
